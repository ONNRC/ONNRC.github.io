Online Neuroscience Network Research Center

Welcome to ONNRC

The Online Neuroscience Network Research Center (ONNRC) is a newly established initiative aimed at advancing neuroscience research through collaboration, innovation, and connectivity. Our mission is to bring together researchers, scientists, and enthusiasts from around the world to share knowledge, resources, and ideas that deepen our understanding of the brain and its complexities.

We are in our beginning steps, laying the foundation for a vibrant community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of neuroscience. Whether you’re a researcher, student, or professional, we invite you to join us as we grow and embark on this exciting journey together.

Meet Our Director

The ONNRC is proudly led by Arshia Vahedi, MD, whose vision and expertise guide our efforts. For more information about our director, visit their LinkedIn profile.
